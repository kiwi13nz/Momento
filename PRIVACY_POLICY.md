# Privacy Policy for Flick

**Last updated: November 28, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how Flick ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
*   **Account Information**: When you use the App, we may collect your User ID and authentication details provided through our backend service.
*   **User Content**: We collect the photos and images you explicitly choose to upload or capture using the App.

### 1.2 Information Automatically Collected
*   **Device Information**: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
*   **Usage Data**: We collect data about how you interact with the App, such as features used and time spent.
*   **Crash Data**: We use Sentry to collect error logs and crash reports to improve App stability. This may include stack traces and device state information at the time of a crash.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
*   Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
*   Enable photo sharing and event participation features.
*   Authenticate users and secure your account.
*   Send push notifications (if you opt-in) regarding app activity.
*   Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with the App.
*   Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities.

## 3. App Permissions

The App requires the following permissions to function:

*   **Camera**: Used to capture photos directly within the App for events.
*   **Photo Library (Read/Write)**: Used to upload existing photos and save captured/edited photos to your device's gallery.
*   **Notifications**: Used to send you alerts about event updates and app activity.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:

*   **Supabase**: We use Supabase for backend services, database, and authentication. Their privacy policy can be found at: [https://supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
*   **Sentry**: We use Sentry for error tracking and performance monitoring. Their privacy policy can be found at: [https://sentry.io/privacy/](https://sentry.io/privacy/)
*   **Expo**: We use Expo for development and push notification services. Their privacy policy can be found at: [https://expo.dev/privacy](https://expo.dev/privacy)

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information and content for as long as necessary to provide the services you have requested, or for other essential purposes such as complying with our legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our policies.

## 6. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no internet or electronic storage system is 100% secure.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
